About Us

U&I is a volunteer-driven charitable organization based in Bangalore, India. This platform is used by NGOs to get the help they need from donors. This helps empower NGOs to receive goods in good condition (clothes,laptops etc.) for the betterment of the community they work with while giving the donors a chance to responsibly give away their unwanted/excess items.

Why you should donate

By donating to our general fund, this enables us to wisely use the resources where the greatest need is at any particular time. By delivering help on the ground through our project partnerships we can help change lives by meeting their basic needs.Our projects are designed to empower greater self-sufficiency, new hope and a more sustainable future.
